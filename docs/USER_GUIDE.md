# User Guide - Seyyal Billing System

## 1. Getting Started
1.  Open the application in your browser.
2.  Enter your **Employee ID** and **Password**.
3.  Click **Login**. You will be redirected to your specific dashboard based on your role.

---

## 2. Biller Guide (Point of Sale)
**Goal:** Process sales and manage customers.

*   **Create Bill:**
    1.  Go to **POS / Billing**.
    2.  Search for a product by name or selecting from the list.
    3.  Adjust quantity.
    4.  Select an existing customer or create a new one.
    5.  Check the total and click **Confirm & Print**.
    6.  A PDF invoice will be generated.
*   **Customer Management:**
    *   Navigate to **Customers** to view or add new customer details.
*   **View Sales:**
    *   Check **My Sales** to see your total revenue for the day.

---

## 3. Inventory Manager Guide
**Goal:** Maintain product catalog and stock levels.

*   **Add Product:**
    1.  Go to **Add Product**.
    2.  Enter Name, Brand, Buying Price, Selling Price.
    3.  Upload an image (optional).
    4.  Save.
*   **Add Stock:**
    1.  Go to **Stock Entry**.
    2.  Select a product.
    3.  Enter the quantity of new items received.
    4.  Submit to update inventory.
*   **Low Stock Alerts:**
    *   Check **Alerts** to see items running low (Red highlight).

---

## 4. Manager Guide
**Goal:** Oversee store performance and rules.

*   **Dashboard:** View real-time Total Sales, Orders, and Active Staff.
*   **Discounts:**
    *   **Global Discount:** Set a store-wide percentage off (e.g., Seasonal Sale).
    *   **Customer Discount:** Assign special rates to loyal customers.
*   **Reports:** Access detailed sales reports by date range or biller.

---

## 5. Admin Guide
**Goal:** System administration.

*   **Employee Management:**
    *   **Add Employee:** Create/Register new staff accounts.
    *   **Manage Roles:** Promote/Demote staff (e.g., Biller -> Manager).
    *   **Deactivate:** Disable access for former employees.
*   **System Stats:** View technical health and high-level business metrics.
